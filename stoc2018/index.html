TCS: Looking
into the Future

1 - 4 PM

June 29th, 2018

Los Angeles, CA


TCS has made major contributions to many areas such as Quantum Computing, Computational Biology, Cryptography, Advertisement auction design via the lens of algorithms and providing formal scientific frameworks. These foundational advances have played a huge role in major societal impact via search engine technologies, e-commerce, secure communications, DNA sequencing etc.

Join us for a half-day workshop of multiple 30-minute speakers to celebrate the 60th birthday of Vijay Vazirani.

Workshop


Vijay Vazirani  has been a leader in the field for many years, with a broad research agenda, and a large set of collaborators, co-authors and students in TCS.

This will be a half-day workshop to celebrate Vijay, his career and contributions to the field, and influence on other TCS researchers, both professionally and personally, on the occasion of his 60th birthday, by bringing together several pioneers in the field to speak about their vision for the future of theoretical computer science and the emerging areas in which we can see further impact in the next decade. What ties all these amazing people together is of course their collaboration with Vijay Vazirani, who himself has made foundational contributions to graph matching theory, complexity theory, approximation algorithms, randomized algorithms, algorithmic game theory, online ad auctions and computability of market equilibria.
