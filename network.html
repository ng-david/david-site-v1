Network Infrastructure

Genesys Impact is a progressive information technology company specializing in the design and installation of low voltage systems that support large facilities and campuses. We are comprised of technically qualified and dedicated managers, technical professionals and field experts who possess a strong commitment to excellence, and have implemented multiple complex low voltage building systems that support facility voice, data, video, security, audio-video, server and network infrastructure, building automation and control systems.

Experience and Past Performance. Genesys Impact is experienced with the implementation of projects of this size and scope. We have implemented thousands of voice, data, and video outlets and miles of CAT6/Fiber/ISP-OSP cable. We have also successfully managed and staffed projects that had up to thirty technicians with multiple teams assigned to various tasks.

Manufacturer Certified. Our installation technicians have been trained and certified by all major cable suppliers and manufacturers. We provide, install, and warrant cabling products and components manufactured by Superior Modular Products, Hubbel, Systimax, CommScope (Uniprise), Ortronics, Panduit, and other major cabling manufacturers. We will maintain manufacturer/brand integrity by installing and warranting systems and components as specified in RFP.

No Learning Curve. Our installation crews have extensive experience with the products we provide and install, and have more than fifteen years of experience installing these systems and components for hundreds of clients.

No Impact on Business Operations. Our technicians have extensive experience working in active office environments and operations. On a daily basis, our technicians install and cutover new cabling systems and components with no impact on business operations. Our technicians fully understand strict requirements for safety, security, and cleanliness in an active business environment.
