Success Stories

Contracts Information Management System

Customer

Consulting firm that provides a vast array of professional services including translation, interpretation, transcription and training for government and commercial clients.

Problem

Insufficient contracts information management legacy system incapable of maintaining complex contracts activities.

  • Contracts activities were not reported on time.
  • Longer time and extended process for activities reporting.
  • System lacks efficiency and common discrepancy between actual activities and activities reported.
  • Vulnerable system with common security threats.

Vision

An efficient, secure system capable of managing extensive contracts activities, services personnel and related time and expenses for complex contracts with multiple and global locations; serving government and commercial entities.

Solution

A modernized contracts information management system built on the Windows platform using the latest Microsoft .NET Framework 4.6 technology and Microsoft Azure SQL. The modernized system is capable of tracking all contract extensive activities with an added reporting component to generate several types of reports including: Billing Reports, Labor Reports, Subcontractor Reports, Timesheet Reports and Expenses Reports. Genesys Impact engineers and developers worked concurrently to complete all project components. IT and database professionals constructed and tested the databases, including a disaster recovery site. Software and web developers created the dashboard, reports and security protocols. Genesys Impact provided the customer with a technologically advanced system that incorporated database architecting/setup/implementation/management, management services, technical knowledge, training, maintenance, and upgrades. and information system requirements.

Outcome

  • A custom software solution based on a web application accessible over the Internet in a secured environment providing all users with access employing SSL authentication.
  • A web based management application that will track all customer contracts activities including the task ordersâ€™(TOs), the cases, the budgets, the expenses, the payrolls and the billing.
  • A contracts management system that tracks employee profiles, their assignments and their daily timesheets which are being processed through the system after site supervisor approval.
  • Reporting component of contracts management system that generates both dashboard-style high-level reports as well as more general dashboard (detailed) reports which can be reviewed by top-level management or assigned to different departments.
